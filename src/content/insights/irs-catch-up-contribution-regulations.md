## What Changed

The IRS has released final regulations addressing catch-up contributions under qualified retirement plans. These rules provide clarity on several issues that have created uncertainty for plan sponsors.

### Key Takeaways

- **Effective Dates**: Most provisions apply to plan years beginning after December 31, 2025
- **Roth Requirements**: High earners must make catch-up contributions on a Roth basis
- **Administrative Relief**: Transition rules provide flexibility for plan amendments

### Next Steps for Plan Sponsors

Review your plan documents and work with your recordkeeper to ensure compliance with the new requirements.
